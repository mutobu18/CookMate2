Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk eggs and milk.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat pan and pour batter.
  5. Cook until golden on both sides. Serve warm.
