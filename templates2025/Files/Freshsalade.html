Fresh Salade

Steps to Follow

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Toss together in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and season with salt & pepper.
  4. Serve fresh and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper
