Veg grilled chiken

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 small zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 small red onion (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the spice oil mixture on both sides.
  4. In another bowl, toss the sliced vegetables with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes on each side or until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  6. At the same time, grill the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally until tender and slightly charred.
  7. Optional: Drizzle the grilled vegetables with balsamic vinegar before serving for extra flavor.
  8. Slice the grilled chicken and serve it alongside the grilled vegetables. Enjoy hot!
