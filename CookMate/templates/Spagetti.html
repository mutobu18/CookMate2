Spagetti Bolognes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté for 2-3 minutes until soft.
  4. Add minced beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  5. Stir in the grated carrot, chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  8. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
