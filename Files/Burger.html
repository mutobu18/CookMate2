Chiken Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground chicken, chopped onion, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until combined.
  2. Form the mixture into burger patties (about 4-5 patties).
  3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through.
  5. Toast the burger buns in the same skillet for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Assemble the burger: Place the chicken patty on the bottom bun, add cheese (if desired), and top with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments.
  7. Cover with the top bun and serve immediately.
