Fresh Salade

Steps

  1. Wash and chop vegetables (lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, etc.).
  2. Slice fruits (apples, grapes, or oranges) if using fruits.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar to make the dressing.
  4. Combine the vegetables and fruits in a large bowl.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until well mixed.
  6. Optionally, add nuts, seeds, or croutons for extra crunch.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
